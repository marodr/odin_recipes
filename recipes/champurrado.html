Champurrado

Descripton

This is third recipe from Mexico. This is a drink usually served during the winter holidays.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 Mexican chocolate tablet (like Abuelita)
  • 1/2 cup of cold water
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 can evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Combine 2 cups of water and cinnamon in a saucepan. Boil for 7 minutes.
  2. Drop in the chocolate and stir for 5 minutes
  3. Combine cornstarch and cold water in a container and mix well
  4. Stir evaporated milk and sugar into the chocolate.
  5. Add the cornstarch mix and stir.
  6. Cook until thickened and serve hot
