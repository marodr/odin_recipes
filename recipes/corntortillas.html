Corn Tortillas

Descripton

This is also a recipe from Mexico. This is a staple of Mexican cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups of masa harina
  • 1 1/8 cups of water

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix masa harina and hot water until it beocmes dough
  2. Cover dough with plastic wrap and allow to stand for 30 minutes
  3. Preheat a cast iron to medium heat
  4. Divide dough into balls. Press each ball of dough
  5. Place tortilla in preheated pan until slightly puffy and then turn tortilla
  6. Remove tortilla after 30 seconds and repeat with the other dough balls
